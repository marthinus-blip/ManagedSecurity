# Strategy Persistence Rule

To ensure continuity and prevent losing track of the project strategy across sessions, the following steps MUST be followed at the start of every session:

1.  **Read the Roadmap**: View `.next.md` to understand the current high-level status and the next immediate goals.
2.  **Review the History**: View `ForTheAI/Journal.md` to catch up on the most recent design decisions and technical changes.
3.  **Respect the Truths**: View `ForTheAI/Truths.md` to remind yourself of the core architectural "laws" (e.g., Zero-Allocation, NativeAOT compliance).
4.  **Update as You Go**: Whenever a major feature is implemented, a bug is fixed, or the strategy changes, update both `.next.md` and `ForTheAI/Journal.md` accordingly.

This ensures that the "Technical Mysteries" of the project are preserved and the momentum is never lost.
